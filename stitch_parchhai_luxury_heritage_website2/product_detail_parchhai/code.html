Description & stylingadd

A relaxed straight-cut kurti hand-block printed in deep indigo Ajrakh with fine geometric stars. Pair it with wide-leg cotton trousers and a printed stole, or layer over denim for an everyday edit.

Fabric & careadd

100% Mul cotton, naturally dyed with indigo and madder. Gentle hand wash separately in cold water, dry in shade, iron on low. Natural dyes may settle gently with wash — a sign of authenticity.

Fit & sizingadd

Relaxed regular fit. Model is 5'7" and wears a size S. If you are between sizes, we recommend sizing down for a closer fit. See the size guide for full measurements.

Shipping & returnsadd

Dispatched in 24–48 hours. Free shipping on orders over ₹1,999. 7-day easy returns and exchanges on unworn items with tags intact.

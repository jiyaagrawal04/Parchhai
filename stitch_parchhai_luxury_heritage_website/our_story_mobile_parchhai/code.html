Legacy & Loom

The intersection of ancestry and silhouette.

"We do not just make garments; we curate the shadows of our ancestors into the wardrobes of the modern global citizen."

Parchhai, meaning 'Reflection', was born from a desire to bridge the gap between India's profound textile heritage and the sharp, minimalist lines of contemporary high fashion.

In a world of fast consumption, we move at the pace of the hand-block and the loom. Each piece is a conversation between a master artisan in Rajasthan and a designer in the city.

KUTCH, GUJARAT

The Alchemy of Ajrakh

Ajrakh is a unique form of block printing found in Kutch. The process is laborious, involving 16 distinct stages of washing, dyeing, and printing with natural minerals and vegetable dyes over several weeks.

LUCKNOW, UTTAR PRADESH

Shadows in Thread

Known as 'Shadow Work', Chikankari is a delicate and artfully done hand embroidery. It is one of Lucknow's most ancient and well-known art forms, traditionally created on fine muslin textiles.

CHIPPA, RAJASTHAN

The Earth’s Resist

Dabu printing is an ancient mud-resist hand-block printing technique. The mud resist is made of clay, lime, and gum arabic, which prevents the dye from penetrating the covered areas, resulting in organic, soft-edged motifs.

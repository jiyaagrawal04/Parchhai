The Shadow of Heritage.

DEFINITION

Parchhai (/pərˈtʃʰaɪ/)

In Hindustani, it means shadow or reflection. To us, it represents the silhouette of the past cast upon the textiles of the future. Every block-print and every weave is a reflection of the artisan's soul, preserving a lineage that refuses to fade.

The Artisan's Manifesto

Natural Dye Integrity

We exclusively use plant-based pigments—Indigofera, Madder Root, and Pomegranate peels. Our water-cycles are closed, returning only purity to the earth from which our colors are born.

The Fair-Wage Promise

Every artisan is a shareholder in our journey. We bypass middlemen to ensure 40% higher than industry-standard wages, supporting the education and healthcare of weaving clusters across Kutch and Bagru.
